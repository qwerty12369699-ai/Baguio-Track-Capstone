Bell Church

A serene Chinese temple with colorful architecture and peaceful gardens.

  • 📍 Location: Bell Church Rd, La Trinidad, Benguet
  • 🕕 Best time: Morning or early afternoon
  • 🪙 Admission: Donation-based entry
  • 📌 Highlights: Chinese temple design, flower gardens, quiet meditation spaces

About Bell Church

Bell Church is a Chinese-Filipino temple known for its ornate pagodas, peaceful garden grounds, and welcoming atmosphere. It is a beautiful spot for visitors seeking quiet reflection and cultural immersion.

Nearby Spots

  • 🛕 Colorful temple architecture
  • 🌺 Garden paths and floral displays
  • 📸 Scenic meditation areas
  • 🍃 Nearby La Trinidad vegetable terraces
