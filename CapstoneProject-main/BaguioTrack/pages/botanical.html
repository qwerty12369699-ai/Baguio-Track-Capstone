Botanical Garden

Experience nature, culture, and peace in the heart of Baguio City.

  • 📍 Location: Leonard Wood Road, Baguio City
  • 💰 Entrance Fee: ₱110 (Adult) | ₱27.50 (Student/Senior)
  • 🕕 Opening Hours: 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • 📌 Highlights: Cultural villages • Sculptures • Walking paths

About Botanical Garden

The Botanical Garden in Baguio City is a beautiful mix of native and international plants, sculptures, and peaceful walking paths. It is ideal for easy strolls, photography, and quiet time away from the busy city center.
