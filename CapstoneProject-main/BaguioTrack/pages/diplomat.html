Diplomat Hotel

A historic hillside hotel known for faded grandeur and ghost stories.

  • 📍 Location: Dominican Hill, Diplomat Road, Baguio, 2600 Benguet
  • 🕕 Best time: Daytime visits for photo tours
  • 🪙 Admission: ₱10 (Adult) | ₱5 (Student/Senior)
  • 📌 Highlights: Historic architecture, haunted reputation, panoramic city views

About Diplomat Hotel

Diplomat Hotel is an iconic historic building on Dominican Hill. Once a seminary and later a luxury hotel, it is now known for its atmospheric ruins, grand staircase, chapel, and local legends.
