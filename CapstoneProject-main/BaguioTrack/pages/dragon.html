Dragon Treasure Castle

A fantasy-themed attraction that delivers dramatic architecture and unforgettable photo moments.

  • 📍 Location: Block 8, Irisville Subdivision, Baguio City, Benguet
  • 🕕 Best time: Late afternoon to evening
  • 🪙 Admission: ₱165 (Adult) | ₱132 (Student/Senior)
  • 📌 Highlights: Dragon architecture, fantasy gardens, immersive photo scenes

About Dragon Treasure Castle

Dragon Treasure Castle is a whimsical destination built for dramatic photos and fantasy-inspired sightseeing. The site blends colorful architecture with lush gardens and is a great side trip from central Baguio.
