Welcome to Baguio City

Known as the Summer Capital of the Philippines, Baguio City is famous for its cool climate, beautiful pine forests, and scenic mountain views. Nestled in the Cordillera Mountains, it offers a refreshing escape and a unique blend of nature, culture, and history.

Visitors can enjoy popular attractions, vibrant markets, local cuisine, and colorful festivals while experiencing the city's warm hospitality. Whether you're looking for relaxation, adventure, or cultural experiences, Baguio City is a destination that has something for everyone.

Use Baguio Track as your local companion for discovering the best of the City of Pines — from memorable sights and cultural experiences to smart routes and authentic food finds.

Local Ordinances & Prohibited Acts

Help us keep Baguio clean and orderly. Please avoid the following prohibited acts.

🚭
No Smoking or Vaping

Baguio is a Smoke-Free City. Smoking and vaping in public places are strictly prohibited.

🚮
No Littering

Keep the City of Pines clean. Dispose of your trash in designated bins only.

🏍️
Session Road Restrictions

Motorcycles, tricycles, and bicycles are strictly prohibited along Session Road and other CBD areas.

⚡
City-Wide Speed Limits

20 kph in CBD, major roads, and school zones. 30-40 kph on national roads and circumferential roads.

🚶
No Jaywalking

Use designated pedestrian crossings and overpasses, especially in the CBD area.

🅿️
No Illegal Parking

Avoid parking on main roads and tow-away zones. Use designated parking areas.
