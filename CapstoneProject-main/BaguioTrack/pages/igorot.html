Igorot Stone Kingdom

A cultural landmark celebrating traditional stone craftsmanship and Cordilleran heritage.

  • 📍 Location: Long Long Road, Pinsao Proper, Baguio City
  • 🕕 Best time: Morning or late afternoon
  • 🪙 Admission: ₱130 (Adult) | ₱100 (Student/Senior)
  • 📌 Highlights: Riprap stone walls, cultural displays, scenic rock gardens

About Igorot Stone Kingdom

Igorot Stone Kingdom showcases traditional Cordillera stonework and cultural practices. The site celebrates the art of stacking stones without cement and provides insight into local building traditions and hillside landscaping.

Nearby Spots

  • 🪨 Rustic stone walls and cultural displays
  • 🌿 Nature trails through Pinsao Proper
  • 📸 Authentic heritage photo opportunities
  • 🍽️ Nearby cafés and local food stalls
