Landmark

Lions Head

A dramatic mountain carving that welcomes travelers along Kennon Road.

  • 📍 Location: Kennon Road, near Barangay Calasungay, Tuba, Benguet
  • 🕕 Best time: Early morning or afternoon drive
  • 🪙 Admission: Free roadside landmark
  • 📌 Highlights: Iconic lion sculpture, scenic mountain photo stop

About Lions Head

The Lion’s Head is one of the most photographed roadside landmarks in northern Luzon. Carved into the mountainside along Kennon Road, it marks the gateway to Baguio City and offers a memorable stop for visitors heading to the city.

Getting There

By Jeepney: Take the jeepney with the route: BAGUIO PLAZA-CAMP 6 V.V.

  • Board from Baguio Plaza or Shopper's Lane near Center Mall
  • Tell the driver: 'Lions Head, please.'
  • Travel time: approximately 20–30 minutes

  • 🛣️ Camp John Hay and Baguio city proper
