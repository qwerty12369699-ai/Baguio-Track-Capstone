The Mansion

Discover the historic summer residence of the Philippine President.

  • 📍 Location: Leonard Wood Road, Baguio City
  • 💰 Entrance Fee: Free (Photo area only)
  • 🕕 Best Time: Morning for cooler weather & clearer views
  • 📌 Highlights: Historical landmark • Grand gates • Scenic gardens • Presidential residence

About The Mansion

The Mansion is one of Baguio’s most iconic landmarks. Built in 1908, it serves as the official summer residence of the President of the Philippines. With elegant Spanish-inspired architecture, wide gardens, and its famous grand gates, it remains one of the most photographed landmarks in the city.

Nearby Places

  • 🌲 Wright Park – Right across The Mansion
  • ☕ Amare La Cucina – 2 mins drive
  • 🏞 Camp John Hay – 5 mins drive
