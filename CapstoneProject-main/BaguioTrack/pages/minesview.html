Mines View Park

A stunning overlook of Benguet’s valleys and mountains.

  • 📍 Location: Mines View Road, Baguio City
  • 💰 Entrance Fee: ₱10 (Adult) | ₱5 (Student/Senior)
  • 🕕 Opening Hours: 6:00 AM – 8:00 PM
  • 📌 Highlights: Viewing deck • Souvenirs • Photos with Igorot attire

About Mines View Park

Mines View Park is a top tourist spot offering panoramic views of Itogon and the Cordillera mountains. Visitors enjoy the viewing deck, local crafts, food stalls, and classic Baguio activities.

Nearby Attractions

  • 🥤 Good Shepherd – 2 mins walk
  • 🧵 Souvenir Shops
  • 🐴 Horseback Photos
