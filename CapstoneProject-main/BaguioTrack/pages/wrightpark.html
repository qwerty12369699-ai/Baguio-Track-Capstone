Park

Wright Park

A popular leisure park with horseback trails, gardens, and cool Baguio air.

  • 📍 Location: The Mansion, Romulo Dr, Baguio, Benguet
  • 🕕 Best time: Morning and late afternoon
  • 🪙 Admission: Free entry, horse ride fees apply
  • 📌 Highlights: Horseback riding, pine-lined walks, and garden viewpoints

About Wright Park

Wright Park is a family-friendly spot famous for horseback riding along the famous “stairway to heaven” and relaxing among pine trees. It sits beside The Mansion and is a quick stroll from the city’s main attractions.

Nearby Spots

  • 🏛️ The Mansion
  • 🌲 Burnham Park
  • 📸 Garden Club of Baguio
  • 🍽️ Local cafés along Romulo Drive
